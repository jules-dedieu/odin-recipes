Confit de Canard

Description

One of the best food coming from the South West of France, a confit de canard is a duck leg that is cooked in duck fat.

Ingredients

  • 1 duck leg
  • 1 duck fat
  • 1 potatoes
  • 1 rosemary

    • Steps

    1. Wash the duck leg and pat it dry.
    2. Rub the duck leg with rosemary and salt.
    3. Put the duck leg in the duck fat and cook it for 1 hour at 160Â°C.
    4. Let the duck leg rest for 1 hour.
    5. Cut the potatoes and cook them in the duck fat.
    6. Serve the duck leg with the potatoes.
