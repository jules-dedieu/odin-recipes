Cassoulet

Description

One of the best food coming from the beautiful city of Castelnaudary, a cassoulet is a dish made with duck leg, beans, and sausage.

Ingredients

  • 1 duck leg
  • 1 duck fat
  • 1 beans
  • 1 sausage

    • Steps

    1. Cook the duck leg in the duck fat.
    2. Cook the beans and sausage in the duck fat.
    3. Serve the duck leg with the beans and sausage.
    4. Enjoy!
    5. Repeat.
